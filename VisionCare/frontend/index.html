VisionCare 2.0

A Multi-Modal Clinical Decision Support System for Heart Failure and Mortality Prediction

Macro AUC: 0.8105 SYMILE-MIMIC Dataset IIIT Kottayam BTP

🧠 Model Architecture

  • CXR Encoder: ConvNeXt-Tiny (768-D)
  • ECG Encoder: Custom 1D-CNN (256-D)
  • Labs Encoder: 2-layer MLP (64-D)
  • Fusion: Cross-Attention Gated Fusion
  • Targets: Mortality + Heart Failure

📊 Performance

  • Macro AUC: 0.8105
  • Mortality AUC: 0.8022
  • Heart Failure AUC: 0.8189
  • Training Set: 10,000 admissions
  • Validation Set: 750 admissions

📋 Dataset

  • Source: SYMILE-MIMIC (PhysioNet)
  • Modalities: CXR + ECG + Labs
  • Total Admissions: 11,622
  • Mortality Labels: Real (hospital_expire_flag)
  • HF Labels: Clinical proxy (Edema+Cardio)

🔬 XAI Features

  • Grad-CAM heatmaps (CXR)
  • Modality contribution gates
  • RAG-powered AI assistant
  • AHA/ESC guideline citations
  • Per-class AUC reporting
